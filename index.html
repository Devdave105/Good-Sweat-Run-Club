Community Standards

We Run by a Code.

Every member upholds the standards that make Good Sweat exceptional. Read our guidelines before joining.

Rules & Guidelines

Please read carefully before joining Good Sweat Run Club Uyo.

  1. Arrive at least 10 minutes before the scheduled run start — lateness disrupts the entire group.
  2. Respect all pace groups. Never pressure another member to run faster or slower than their ability.
  3. Proper running attire is required. No jeans, dress shoes, or inappropriate clothing at any run.
  4. No harassment, discrimination, or disrespectful conduct toward any member or the public at any time.
  5. Stay on the designated run route unless redirected by a team lead.
  6. You are responsible for your own health and safety. Run within your personal limits always.
  7. Follow all instructions from team leads and run coordinators without argument during the run.
  8. Photography is encouraged but always get consent before photographing others.
  9. Do not litter. Help us keep the streets of Uyo clean and beautiful.
  10. Members who consistently disrupt the community will be removed after a fair warning.
  11. Stay hydrated. Always bring water especially for distances beyond 5km.
  12. In the event of an injury, stop immediately and alert your nearest team lead.
