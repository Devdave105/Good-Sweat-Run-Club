Community Standards

We Run by a Code.

Every member upholds the standards that make Good Sweat exceptional. Read our guidelines before you join.

Rules & Guidelines

Please read carefully before joining Good Sweat Run Club Uyo.

  1. Arrive at least 10 minutes before the run start — lateness disrupts the entire group.
  2. Respect all pace groups. Never pressure anyone to run faster or slower than their ability.
  3. Proper running attire is required. No jeans, dress shoes, or inappropriate clothing.
  4. No harassment, discrimination, or disrespectful conduct toward any member or the public.
  5. Stay on the designated route unless redirected by a team lead.
  6. You are responsible for your own safety. Run within your personal limits at all times.
  7. Follow all instructions from team leads and run coordinators without argument.
  8. Photography is encouraged but always get consent before photographing others.
  9. Do not litter. Help us keep the streets of Uyo clean and beautiful.
  10. Members who repeatedly disrupt the community will be removed after fair warning.
  11. Stay hydrated. Always bring water, especially for distances beyond 5km.
  12. In the event of an injury, stop immediately and alert your nearest team lead.
