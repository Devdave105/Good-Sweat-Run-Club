Who We Are

About Good Sweat

Good Sweat is a growing community of runners built on movement, connection, and consistency. What started as a simple idea has grown into a space where people come together to run, connect, and become part of something bigger than themselves.

Our Story

In a city like Uyo, where there was no active running culture, it all started with a simple thought — we need something like this here.

What began as a bold step by one person quickly turned into a movement. At first, it wasn't easy. It required consistency, awareness, and creating a safe, enjoyable experience.

But people started showing up. And they kept showing up.

From about 15 people to over 400 runners in just a few months.
